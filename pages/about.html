About Me

I am an undergraduate student studying User Experience & Interaction Design at Drexel University with a strong foundation in interaction design, information architecture, and user- centered design principles. Experienced in conducting user research, creating wireframes and prototypes, and usability testing. Passionate about creating intuitive, user-friendly designs that enhance user satisfaction and engagement.

What drew me to UX specifically is the opportunity to bridge the gap between technology and human experience. Functionality alone isn’t enough; usability is everything. And UX design allows me to translate how people think and behave into intuitive, thoughtful products that can genuinely improve their lives. It’s a field where problem-solving, creativity, and empathy come together, and knowing that a small design decision can make someone’s day a little easier is what makes this work so fulfilling.


When I’m not deep in design mode, you’ll probably find me sketching, trying out a new recipe, snapping photos of little everyday moments, or catching up on comics. I love having creative outlets outside of UX, it keeps things fresh and inspires me in unexpected ways.
